'''Randall''' was a former CFO of [[Dunder Mifflin Paper Company|Dunder Mifflin]], who resigned early on in the series (some point before "[[Sexual Harrassment]]") and was replaced by [https://theoffice.fandom.com/wiki/David_Wallace David Wallace] . He secretly had a sexual relationship with his secretary, which she later revealed to the other staff, "just to be a bitch" (in the words of [[Todd Packer]]). ("[[Sexual Harrassment]]") His resignation came as a result of this.
