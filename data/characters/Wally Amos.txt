{{Actor|image = FamousAmos.jpg|Actor = Wally Amos|character = Himself|dateofbirth = July 1, 1936|placeofbirth = Tallahassee, FL, USA}}Wally Amos, the founder of Famous Amos cookies, appears in the episode "[[Tallahassee]]". He makes a guest appearance at Sabre corporate headquarters and attempts to make a speech, but he soon figures out that he is just there because of the cookies.
