}}'''Mark''' is a fictional character played by Jeffrey Muller in the television series ''[[The Office]]''. He was Jim's roommate until Jim moved to Stamford. It is unclear whether Jim moved back in with Mark after returning to Scranton in "[[The Merger]]".

==Season 2==
Mark is [[Jim Halpert|Jim]]'s roommate who is very interested in meeting Jim's co-workers based on the stories he has heard. He is especially interested in meeting [[Dwight Schrute|Dwight]] because he doesn't believe that Dwight could be real. Mark is very impressed when he meets Dwight and realizes that Jim was telling the truth.
