[[File:Screen Shot 2016-03-31 at 1.03.08 AM.png|thumb|300px|right|Sconesy Cider]]'''Sconesy Cider''', is a fictional character in the television series ''[[The Office|The Office]]''.
==Season 7==
At [[Cecelia Halpert|Cece]]'s christening, an unnamed woman complains that she didn't get a scone, and that there is only one jug of apple cider. Jim mockingly calls her "Sconesy Cider, baptism reception critic."
