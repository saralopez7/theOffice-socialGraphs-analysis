==Season 4==
Paul Faust is the owner of [[Disaster Kits Limited]], one of the other businesses operating in [[Scranton Business Park]]. Some people call him "Cool Guy Paul". In the episode ''[[Chair Model]]'', [[W.B. Jones Heating & Air]] goes under renovations and their construction vehicles take up parking spaces. As a result of this, the leaders of the "Five Families" of the business park is called to discuss the parking problem. The problem is resolved quickly.
