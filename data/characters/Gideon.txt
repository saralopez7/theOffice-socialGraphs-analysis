Gideon (Barak Hardley) is a part-time dock worker in the [[Dunder Mifflin]] [[warehouse]]. His first appearance is in "[[Lotto]]", as an applicant for a position in the warehouse (after the existing workers quit after winning the lottery). He is a PhD candidate, and is studying North America's diminishing blue collar workforce. Gideon also works as a teacher Wednesday through Friday.
