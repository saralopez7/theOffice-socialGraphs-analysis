== Season 1 ==

Jerry works in the warehouse. During the [[basketball]] game between the office and the warehouse, [[Michael Scott|Michael]] called a "flagrant personal intentional foul" on Jerry to end the game, announcing the team from the office as the winners. [[Darryl Philbin|Darryl]], [[Roy Anderson|Roy]], and [[Lonnie]] however were none-too-receptive with Michael's decision and, through intimidation, had it reversed.
