'''Miserly Man''' was an applicant for the job as Regional Manger of [[Dunder Mifflin-Sabre]]. He was portrayed by Warren Buffett.

During his interview he appears greedy and constantly asks about the perks and benefits he would receive should he be given the job. He is the only candidate not interviewed by the cameras{{7x25}}.
